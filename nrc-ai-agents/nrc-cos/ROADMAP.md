# NRC-COS Roadmap

This roadmap describes how NRC-COS evolves as a system — what layer comes next and why — not a delivery schedule with dates.

## Completed

**Phase 0 — Philosophy.** Frozen. Brand Philosophy, Design Constitution, Design Language, and North Star establish why NRC exists, the enforceable laws every decision must satisfy, the resulting creative character, and the one-page compression of all three.

**Phase 1 — Principles.** Frozen. Eleven documents translate Philosophy into enduring, testable standards across Communication (Visual Language, Motion Language, Animation Principles, Interaction Principles), Expression (Typography, Spacing, Color, Voice, Representation), and Universal Application (Accessibility, Performance Standards).

**Phase 2 — Frameworks.** Frozen. Discovery, Concept Exploration, Narrative, Proof, and Invitation Frameworks were written in dependency order, each independently reviewed, revised, and frozen.

**Product Layer Architecture and Product Lifecycle.** Frozen. Define what a Product is, what it owns, how it derives from the Framework chain, and how it moves from creation through freeze and eventual retirement.

**Products 001–005.** Constructed and validated. Product 001 — Founder Introduction — completed full architectural review and revision as the repository's canonical reference. Products 002–005 — Service Introduction, Case Study, Discovery Call, Selected Work — validated constitutional stability across four further, materially different communication situations.

**v1.0 Validation Report.** Complete. Confirms the frozen constitution required no amendment across five independently derived Products.

## Current

**Implementation.** The constitutional and Product layers are complete; the current phase is realizing what they've already decided, in a real medium and technology. The first implementation target is Product 001 — Founder Introduction, both because it is the repository's canonical reference and because it is the one Product to have completed independent architectural review in full.

## Future

**Further Products.** New situations will continue to produce new Products, each running the same five-Framework chain independently, with the same discipline whether it's the sixth Product or the six-hundredth. No new Product is currently planned or implied by this roadmap.

**Pattern extraction.** The Patterns layer stays empty until a Product's specific decision has been proven independently, in a second, genuinely different Product. This has not yet happened for any decision in Products 001–005, and nothing in this roadmap treats it as due.

**Repository maturity.** Once implementation begins, the full governance apparatus already defined — the Creative Lifecycle's promotion gates, the Creative Debt review cadence, quarterly Philosophy and Principles audits — moves from dormant to active. ADRs begin accumulating in `decisions/` as real choices get made, rather than only as backfilled history.

**Long-term governance.** The Retirement Model remains dormant until something is actually superseded. One architectural observation is being monitored, not acted on: Product 005 required an interpretive extension of Narrative Framework to accommodate a non-linear visitor experience. This occurred once. It is retained as something to watch for recurrence, not queued as planned revision work — consistent with this system's standard that a single instance is architectural information, not grounds for changing the constitution.
